# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

ActTrack is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors and users. We are dedicated to maintaining a community that is free from harassment, discrimination, and abuse.

## Our Standards

We expect members of our community to:

### Be Respectful
- Treat all people with kindness and respect
- Listen actively to others' perspectives
- Acknowledge and respect differences in opinion
- Avoid dismissive or condescending language

### Be Professional
- Keep discussions focused on the project
- Use constructive criticism
- Acknowledge mistakes and learn from them
- Support others' learning and growth

### Be Inclusive
- Welcome people of all backgrounds
- Use welcoming language
- Respect people's privacy
- Promote diversity of thought

### Uphold Core Values
- Maintain the non-commercial, educational purpose
- Support open-source principles
- Honor intellectual property rights
- Give proper attribution

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are NOT acceptable in our community:

- Harassment, intimidation, or bullying
- Discrimination based on any characteristic
- Aggressive or hostile language
- Sexual harassment or inappropriate comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any form of abuse or violence
- Attempted commercialization or monetization
- Removing or misrepresenting attribution
- Copyright infringement or license violation

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document the incident** - Note what happened, when, and who was involved
2. **Report it** - Contact the project maintainer with details
3. **Provide context** - Explain why you believe it violates the code of conduct

We will take all reports seriously and investigate appropriately while maintaining confidentiality.

## Enforcement

Violations of this code of conduct may result in:
- Warning or reprimand
- Temporary suspension from the project
- Permanent removal from the community
- Other actions deemed necessary

The severity of consequences will be determined on a case-by-case basis.

## Appeal Process

If you believe a decision was unjust, you may:
1. Request a review of the decision
2. Provide additional context or evidence
3. Present your perspective respectfully

We will re-evaluate the situation fairly and equally.

## Community Guidelines

### For Contributors
- Follow [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) guidelines
- Write clear, kind commit messages
- Be open to feedback and suggestions
- Help others who are learning

### For Users
- Report bugs clearly and constructively
- Ask questions respectfully
- Share feedback professionally
- Respect others' time and effort

### For Maintainers
- Enforce this code consistently and fairly
- Make decisions transparently
- Explain reasoning behind moderation
- Welcome all good-faith contributions

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant v2.1 and tailored for ActTrack's educational mission.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, forums, etc.)
- Official project communications
- Community interactions related to ActTrack
- Representation of the project in public

## Our Commitment

As maintainers and contributors, we commit to:
- Creating a safe environment
- Addressing violations promptly
- Treating all members fairly
- Continuously improving our community

By participating in ActTrack, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.
